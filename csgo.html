This is Counter Strike Global Offensive (CSGO).

In my opinion it is the third best game.


CSGO is a game where you can play as a soldier or a terrorist in different modes. You can shoot different guns, throw grenades, and defuse bombs. You can also play online with other people and rank up your skills. The game is very competitive and exciting, and you can customize your weapons and stickers. CSGO is like a realistic war game, but you can respawn and try again.
