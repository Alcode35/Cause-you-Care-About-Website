This is Red Dead Redemption 2.

In my opinion it is the second best game.


Red Dead Redemption 2 is a game where you can play as an outlaw in the Wild West. You can ride horses, shoot guns, hunt animals, and rob trains. You can also make choices that affect the story and your relationships with other characters. The game is very realistic and beautiful, and you can explore a huge map with different regions and climates. Red Dead Redemption 2 is like a movie, but you are the main character.
